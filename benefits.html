Lighter, Stronger, Longer-standing landmarks

In 1947, NorthEast Solite products were originally designed to solve one problem; Concrete was too heavy.

For over 65 years we have proudly produced agregate fillers that are lighter, stronger and, the real benefit, longer lasting than any of today's common concrete fillers.

Our Process

1.

First we take raw shale rock and send it through an intense heating process. The rock expands as it is heated until its pores are much larger. As a result the aggregate is much lighter.

Think of it as stone popcorn -- We heat up the rock until it expands. Which weighs more, a bag of kernels or a bag of popped corn?

2.

Our new expanded shale is then used to build everything from roads to roofs. When it is mixed into concrete the pores trap water, reducing evaporation from inside of the rock.

3.

As the concrete cures, the additional water available benefits the internal chemical reaction -- curing. This makes the concrete stiffer, harder, and more efficient.

The more internal water in the curing process the longer the process lasts, which creates a stronger structure.
